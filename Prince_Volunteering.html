In addition to being in the work field, I am also an avid volunteer, at various school and community events. As of now, I have completed over 30 hours of volunteering, and am ecstatic to continue this journey. Aforementioned, I enjoy working with young kids, which has led me to volunteer at my local elementary school, John Green Elementary School. Some of the events that I have coordinated include Sadia Science Fair Night, Family Art Night and John Green 10th Anniversary and Multicultural Fair. Through these opportunities, I have been able to collaborate with many parents, who lead the volunteering at the school and provide my effort to create fun-filled events. In addition, as a member of various clubs, I have done many fundraising, and publicizing events, to increase the club reach. Lastly, my latest volunteering expedition has to lead me to be a part of The Lawrence Hall of Science, volunteering team. Out of over 200 applicants, I was selected to be a volunteer for the upcoming summer, in the Ingenuity Challenges section of the hall. As a volunteer of this section, I will be expected to foster an interest in STEM fields for our various visitors and engage them in an iterative engineering design process.
